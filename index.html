WHIPPET'S GANG

Why adopt a Whippet puppy? The Whippet is a very affectionate and sociable dog that will find its place in the home. He needs a lot of love and attention. Don't expect him to sleep quietly in his crate, he will prefer to be warm with you under the covers. Although a thief, he is a very intelligent dog and easy to train. He is a dog that loves to run so he needs to exercise. It is the ideal companion, it will love to accompany you everywhere.

Let me introduce you my dogs

Lewis

Lewis was born on 8 March 2015. He is the head of the siblings. He is very independent and likes to be alone without his brothers and sisters. He loves to laze in the sun in the garden and chase rabbits. He is a very sensitive and loving dog.

Ooper

Ooper was born on 16 August 2018. He is the drama queen of the siblings. He is fussy and often squeals for nothing. But he is a very affectionate and playful dog. He needs a lot of attention to be happy. He loves to hunt lizards and birds.

Toscane

Toscane is the youngest, she was born on 6 December 2022. Although she is still a baby, she already has the character of a chipie. Even if she is the only female, she leads them around and does what she wants when she wants.
